Visit Kharkiv

Enjoy numerous museums, theatres and libraries in this cultural, educational, and industrial centre of Ukraine.

Top three activities to do in Kharkiv

Go for a walk in the local park

The Gorky Park is perfect for picnic, or just chilling out

Attend a show or a concert

The Zhivot or Venom clubs are great places to spend your night out

Eat incredibly delicious food

Enjoy Ukrainian dishes at Trypichchya or a square pizza at Bufet
